Delhi

Famous Attractions

Delhi is home to some of India's most famous landmarks. Here are some must-visit places:

  • 🏛️ India Gate
  • 🏰 Red Fort
  • 🕌 Jama Masjid
  • 🗿 Qutub Minar
  • 🏛️ Rashtrapati Bhavan
  • 🎭 Chandni Chowk (Famous for Street Food!)

Food & Culture

Delhi is known for its diverse food culture. Here are some must-try dishes:

  • 🍲 Butter Chicken
  • 🌮 Chole Bhature
  • 🌯 Parathas from Paranthe Wali Gali
  • 🍛 Rajma Chawal
  • 🥙 Kebab Rolls
  • 🍨 Kulfi & Rabri
